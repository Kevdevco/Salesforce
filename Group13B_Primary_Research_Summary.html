5 Key Strategic Insights

Headline findings and implications for Salesforce

1

AI Is Winning the Admin War, But Losing the Strategy Battle

Evidence: 53% cite admin tasks as top value area, while Customer Retention (53%) and Strategic Decision-Making (35%) are the most "underused" areas.
Salesforce implication: Position Agentforce as a strategic partner, not just an admin assistant. Highlight retention & planning use cases.
2

The Skills Gap Is the #1 Barrier ‚Äî Bigger Than Cost

Evidence: 60% cite lack of skills as a barrier, even among active users.
Salesforce implication: Invest heavily in SME-specific onboarding paths. "Training is the unlock."
3

Data Quality Is the Silent Killer of Trust

Evidence: "Garbage in, garbage out" was the dominant open-text theme.
Salesforce implication: Bundle Data Cloud features as essential prerequisites for AI success.
4

Confidence Follows Usage

Evidence: 100% of heavy users are confident. Non-users are not.
Focus on "Quick Wins" to build early confidence.
5

SMEs Want AI Scaled to Them

Evidence: 67% of SMEs see AI as essential but feel tools are "too big."
Create lightweight "Essentials" AI packages.
